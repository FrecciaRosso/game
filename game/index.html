Meet Joseph! He's a professional astronom. He made his hobby his occupation. Space is not only his job subject, but a dream. One day Joseph conducted some research and suddenly calculated a possibly existing star. It couldn't be seen through the telescope, thus the only way to confirm it's existence is to travel and see it in person. Help Joseph make his way through the space to the desired goal.
Use the corresponding arrow keys to move UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT.
Use the SPACE bar to shoot.
